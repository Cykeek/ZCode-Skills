## Role Summary

> **Product Manager** — Owns product strategy, discovery, and delivery for a defined surface area. Balances user needs, business goals, and technical constraints. Leads cross-functional teams (Eng, Design, Data) without authority. Measures success by outcomes (metrics), not output (features). At Senior+ defines multi-quarter strategy; at Director+ sets org-wide vision and builds PM craft.

---

## Core Responsibilities (ATS-Keyword-Rich Bullet Bank)

### Product Discovery (All Levels)
- Conduct **user research**: **interviews**, **surveys**, **usability testing**, **JTBD**, **diary studies** — synthesize into **opportunity solution trees**
- Perform **quantitative analysis**: **SQL**, **Python/R**, **Mixpanel**, **Amplitude**, **Heap**, **Looker**, **Tableau** — identify **drop-offs**, **correlations**, **segments**
- Design and run **experiments**: **A/B testing**, **multivariate**, **sequential testing**, **power analysis**, **guardrail metrics** — ship **statistically significant** wins
- Execute **competitive intelligence**: **feature audits**, **SWOT**, **market sizing** (TAM/SAM/SOM), **win/loss analysis**, **analyst reports** (Gartner, Forrester)
- Validate **problem-solution fit**: **fake door tests**, **prototypes** (Figma), **concierge MVP**, **wizard of oz** — before engineering investment

### Product Delivery (All Levels)
- Write **PRDs / One-Pagers / Strategy Docs**: problem, goals, non-goals, success metrics, risks, rollout plan, rollback criteria
- Apply **prioritization frameworks**: **RICE**, **ICE**, **WSJF**, **Kano**, **MoSCoW**, **Cost of Delay**, **Opportunity Scoring** — transparent, data-informed
- Build **outcome-oriented roadmaps**: **Now/Next/Later**, **theme-based**, **capacity planning**, **dependency mapping**, **PI Planning** (SAFe)
- Run **Agile/Scrum/Kanban** rituals: sprint planning, refinement, standups, retros, release planning — **predictable delivery**
- Communicate **stakeholder updates**: executive summaries, status reports, decision logs, RFCs, demo days — **no surprises**

### Technical Fluency (Mid+)
- Understand **system architecture**: API design, data models, microservices, event-driven, latency/throughput trade-offs
- Query **data independently**: **SQL** (joins, window functions, CTEs), event tracking (Segment, RudderStack), warehouse (Snowflake, BigQuery)
- Grasp **technical concepts**: REST/GraphQL/gRPC, Auth (OAuth/OIDC), caching, CDN, feature flags (LaunchDarkly), CI/CD
- Think **platform**: APIs, SDKs, webhooks, extensibility, developer experience, ecosystem strategy

### Go-to-Market & Business (Senior+)
- Lead **launch planning**: GTM strategy, positioning, messaging, sales enablement, customer comms, phased rollout
- Own **pricing & packaging**: value-based pricing, tiering, metering, trials, freemium, enterprise contracts
- Manage **lifecycle**: onboarding, activation, retention, expansion, churn reduction, win-back
- Drive **partnerships**: integration strategy, marketplace, co-marketing, OEM, channel partnerships

### Leadership (Group PM+)
- **Manage PMs**: hiring, coaching, career development, performance management, team rituals
- **Cross-functional influence**: Engineering, Design, Data, Marketing, Sales, Support, Legal, Finance
- **Org design**: team topologies, platform vs. stream-aligned, cognitive load, Conway's Law awareness
- **Strategy**: vision setting, portfolio management, resource allocation, investment cases, board prep
