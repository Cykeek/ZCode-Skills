## Role Summary

> **Engineering Manager** — Leads a team of 5-8 engineers: owns people management (hiring, growth, retention), technical leadership (architecture, code quality, debt), and delivery (Agile, roadmap, execution). Partners with Product on strategy; translates business goals into technical plans. At Senior Manager level manages managers; at Director level owns multi-domain strategy and organization design.

---

### People Management (All Levels)
- **Hire & grow** high-performing teams: **source**, **screen**, **interview** (technical, behavioral, system design), **close** candidates; **improve hiring funnel** (pass-through rates, time-to-fill, diversity)

- **Career development**: **growth plans**, **skill matrices**, **mentorship programs**, **sponsorship**, **stretch assignments**, **learning budgets**, **conference attendance**, **internal mobility**
