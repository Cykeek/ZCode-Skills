## Role Summary

> **UX Designer** — Discovers user needs through research, translates insights into intuitive flows and interfaces, and validates designs through testing. Partners with Product and Engineering to ship usable, accessible, and delightful experiences. At Senior+ levels, defines research strategy, drives design system governance, and influences product direction.

---

## Core Responsibilities (ATS-Keyword-Rich Bullet Bank)

### Research & Discovery (All Levels)
- Plan and execute **user research** studies: **user interviews**, **contextual inquiry**, **usability testing** (moderated/unmoderated), **card sorting**, **tree testing**
- Analyze **quantitative data** (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Pendo) and **qualitative insights** to identify **user pain points**, **behavior patterns**, **opportunity areas**
- Develop **personas**, **jobs-to-be-done (JTBD)** frameworks, **empathy maps**, **experience maps** grounded in research evidence
- Conduct **competitive analysis**, **heuristic evaluations**, **cognitive walkthroughs** to benchmark experience quality
- Synthesize findings into **actionable research reports**, **presentation decks**, **video highlight reels** for stakeholder alignment

### Design & Prototyping (All Levels)
- Create **user flows**, **task flows**, **site maps**, **information architecture** diagrams for complex product areas
- Produce **low-fidelity wireframes**, **mid-fidelity mockups**, **high-fidelity prototypes** in **Figma** using **Auto Layout**, **Variants**, **Design Tokens**, **Component Properties**
- Build **interactive prototypes** for **usability testing**, **stakeholder demos**, **engineering handoff** (Figma DevMode, **Figma Prototype**, **Framer**, **ProtoPie**)
- Design **micro-interactions**, **transitions**, **loading states**, **empty states**, **error states** with **motion specifications**
- Apply **WCAG 2.1 AA** standards: **color contrast**, **focus management**, **ARIA labels**, **keyboard navigation**, **screen reader compatibility**

### Design Systems & Consistency (Mid+)
- Contribute to and maintain **design system component library** in **Figma** (foundations: color, typography, spacing, shadows; components: buttons, forms, navigation, data display)
- Define **design tokens** (Figma Tokens, Style Dictionary) for **cross-platform consistency** (web, iOS, Android)
- Document **component usage guidelines**, **accessibility requirements**, **implementation specs** for engineers
- Partner with **Design Systems Engineers** on **Storybook** integration, **component parity** audits

### Collaboration & Product Partnership (All Levels)
- Partner with **Product Managers** on **discovery**, **problem framing**, **opportunity solution trees**, **OKR definition**
- Collaborate with **Engineers** on **design feasibility**, **technical constraints**, **implementation planning**, **design QA**
- Facilitate **design critiques**, **stakeholder reviews**, **cross-functional workshops** (design sprints, journey mapping sessions)
- Present **design rationale** backed by **research evidence**, **business metrics**, **user feedback**
- Write **design documentation**: **PRDs**, **design specs**, **handoff notes**, **decision logs**

### Strategic Leadership (Senior+)
- Define **UX research strategy** and **design vision** for product area or platform
- Establish **design quality metrics**: **task success rate**, **time-to-complete**, **System Usability Scale (SUS)**, **NPS**, **accessibility audit scores**
- Mentor **Junior/Mid designers**; conduct **portfolio reviews**, **career coaching**, **skill development plans**
- Drive **design operations**: **tooling standards**, **file organization**, **naming conventions**, **handoff processes**, **design review cadences**
- Influence **product roadmap** through **strategic research**, **competitive intelligence**, **user voice programs**
